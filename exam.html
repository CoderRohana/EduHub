Entrance Exams

JEE

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is an engineering entrance assessment conducted for admission to various engineering colleges in India.

NEET UG

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET-UG) is the qualifying test for MBBS and BDS programs in Indian medical and dental colleges.

CUET

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is an all-India test for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in central universities across India.

CLAT

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is a centralized national level entrance test for admissions to law universities in India.

GATE

GATE is an entrance exam for candidates who wants to pursue M.Tech (Master of Technology) or ME (Master of Engineering) from IITs.

NEET PG

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Postgraduates (NEET-PG) is the qualifying test for various MD/MS courses.

CAT

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is a computer-based test for admission to graduate management programs in IIMs and other top B-schools in India.

IISER Aptitude Test

Entrance exams for five-year-long BS-MS dual degree programmes in IISER

BITSAT

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) is an entrance exam conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, for admissions to undergraduate engineering courses (BE), Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm) and Msc at its three campuses located at Pilani, Goa and Hyderabad.

ISI Entrance Exam

Entrance exam conducted by ISI Kolkata in order to grant admission into various courses like B.Stat(Hons), B.Math(Hons), etc.

National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Entrance Test

Entrance exam conducted for fashion design programmes offered at both UG and PG levels.

Management Aptitude Test (MAT)

The MAT entrance exam is conducted by the All India Management Association (AIMA) for candidates desiring to pursue MBA/PGDM in India. It is considered the second best option after CAT.
