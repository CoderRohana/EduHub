Exams for Outside India

SAT

The Scholastic Assessment Test, entrance exam for students who wish to pursue undergraduate courses, especially in Canada & US

GRE

The Graduate Record Examination is an entrance exam for pursuing postgraduate (PG) level studies in universities abroad.

GMAT

The Graduate Management Admission Test used for obtaining admissions to B-schools abroad

IELTS

English Proficiency test which measures our english reading,writing,listening and speaking skills.
